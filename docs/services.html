Complete Pooja Services

We perform a wide range of Vedic rituals at Trimbakeshwar, each with its unique significance and purpose for the devotee's well-being.

Kaal Sarp Dosh Pooja

To mitigate the negative effects of planets Rahu and Ketu, bringing stability and success in life.

Narayan Nagbali Pooja

A three-day ritual to resolve issues related to ancestral curses, untimely deaths in the family, and to be blessed with children.

Tripindi Shradh

This ritual is done to bring peace to the souls of ancestors who have been trapped for generations, helping resolve family issues.

Rudrabhishek

A sacred ceremony to worship Lord Shiva in his Rudra form, performed to remove all evils and fulfill one's wishes.

Mahamrityunjay Jap

Chanting of the powerful Mahamrityunjay Mantra for longevity, health, and protection from calamities.

Kumbh Vivah

A ritual performed to nullify Manglik Dosha, ensuring a happy and stable married life.
